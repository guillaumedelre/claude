When writing any output:

- Never use the em dash character (—)
- Use alternatives: colon, hyphen, comma, parentheses, or split into two sentences

Example:

```
Bad:  The service failed — retry after 30 seconds.

Good: The service failed. Retry after 30 seconds.
Good: The service failed: retry after 30 seconds.
Good: The service failed (retry after 30 seconds).
```
